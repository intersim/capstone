How to Create a Loop

Welcome to the Symph Loop Editor!

What's a Loop?

A Loop is a sequence of musical notes that can be added to and repeated in Mixes.

About the Loop Grid

Notes are played from left to right (each cell correlates to one beat).

The pitches ascend from the bottom to top of the grid - the higher the cell, the higher the pitch.

Create Notes

  • Create a Note: Click a cell on the grid.
  • Move a Note: Drag and drop to change pitch or the start time of the note
  • Change Duration of a Note: Click a note to select, then drag its left or right edge to make it sound for longer.
  • Delete a Note: Click a note to select, then click the Delete button

Control Playback

  • Play the Loop: Click the Play button
  • Stop Playing: Click the Stop button

Save a Loop

Click the Save button.
