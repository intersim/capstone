Create a Mix

Mixes allow you to combine Loops in multi-track compositions. Each Mix is 16 measures long and can contain up to 4 tracks.

Add Loop to Mix

To add a loop to a Mix, click Open Loop Bucket. Your Loop Bucket contains all of the Loops you've created or saved to it. Click and drag a loop to copy it from the Loop bucket, then drop it in a measure in one of the Mix's tracks.

Add a New Track

Click the New Track button to add a new track to your composition.

Remove a Loop from Mix

To remove a Loop, click and drag the Loop, and a Trash area will appear in the bottom right corner of the screen. Just drop the Loop in that corner, and it will be removed from your composition.

Deleting a Loop on the Mix page does not remove it from your Loop bucket, just from the Mix.

Playback

Click the button to hear your Mix start playing.

Click the button to stop playback.

Save a Mix

Once you're ready to save your Mix, click the button, fill out details about your Mix in the form, and then click Save.
